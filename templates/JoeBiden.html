The President when you were born was...

Joe Biden

Joe Biden is the current president of the United States, having been inaugurated on January 20, 2021. Biden took office after a tumultuous 2020 election season, which occurred amidst the worst pandemic in a century, racial turmoil, and rampant misinformation from his opponent, Donald Trump. Soon after becoming president, Biden lobbied for and successfully passed a very large stimulus bill to deal with the effects of the recession caused by the COVID-19 pandemic. However, as the third of three enormous stimulus bills passed during the pandemic, it was widely blamed for accelerating inflation, which would reach highs not seen in decades under Biden's administration. Biden also controversially ordered the complete withdrawal of U.S. troops from Afghanistan, which would lead to the collapse of the U.S.-backed Afghan government and the recapture of Kabul by the Taliban. Additionally, Biden has approved significant military aid to Ukraine amidst Russia's invasion of the country. Dogged by low approval ratings, a poor economy at home, and questions about his age, Biden faces off against Trump again in 2024, after overwhelmingly being renominated by his party.
