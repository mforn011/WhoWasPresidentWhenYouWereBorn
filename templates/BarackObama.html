The President when you were born was...

Barack Obama

Barack Obama was president from January 20, 2009 to January 20, 2017. The first African-American president in US history, Obama was confronted with the worst economic climate since the Great Depression soon after taking office. To prevent further worsening of conditions, Obama lobbied for the passage of a large stimulus bill, and signed it into law after it was passed. He also bailed out American automakers on the verge of bankruptcy, and enacted major financial regulations to prevent another similar crisis from occurring. The biggest achievement of his first term was the Affordable Care Act, which enacted significant changes to the American healthcare system. Despite a poor showing by his party in the 2010 midterms, Obama managed to win reelection in 2012. His second term would see the eventual recovery of the US economy, with unemployment at the end of his term the lowest since 2007. His presidency would also see the legalization of same-sex marriage, via the Supreme Court decision Obergefell v. Hodges.
