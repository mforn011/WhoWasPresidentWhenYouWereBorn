The President when you were born was...

Ronald Reagan

Ronald Reagan was president from January 20, 1981 to January 20, 1989. He won resoundingly in 1980 and 1984, defeating Jimmy Carter and Walter Mondale in landslides. To deal with stagflation and recession, Reagan proposed and enacted a series of tax cuts and deregulation, as well as increased interest rates. At the same time, Reagan ended détente with the Soviet Union and ramped up the arms race through increased defense spending. His presidency saw dramatically reduced inflation and unemployment, and renewed confidence in America. Many academics also credit Reagan for contributing to the end of the Cold War through his aggressive foreign policy.
